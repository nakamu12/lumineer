# Lumineer Logo - Gemini Generation Prompt

## Prompt

```
Design a minimal, modern logo icon for "Lumineer" — an AI-powered intelligent course discovery platform that illuminates the path of learning.

Concept: A lighthouse in the background emitting rays of light that fan out toward the viewer, and those rays gracefully transform into the shape of open book pages. The lighthouse represents guidance and illumination. The light beams spreading outward become the two open pages of a book, symbolizing knowledge and learning. This creates a seamless visual fusion: a beacon of light that IS a book. The name "Lumineer" combines "illuminate" and "pioneer."

Design requirements:
- A small, minimal lighthouse silhouette positioned at the center-back (upper center) of the icon
- Two beams of light radiating outward and downward from the lighthouse, fanning out symmetrically
- These two light beams curve and widen to form the shape of two open book pages — the left beam becomes the left page, the right beam becomes the right page
- The transition from light rays to book pages should feel natural and fluid, not forced
- The lighthouse itself should be very simple — just a narrow tower shape with a glowing top
- Clean, geometric, minimal style. NOT realistic, ornate, detailed, or hand-drawn
- Flat design with a smooth gradient from teal to blue across the shape
- Primary gradient: from teal (#2A9D8F) through teal-bright (#14B8A6) to blue (#3B82F6). The lighthouse can be darker teal, the light/pages transition to brighter teal and blue
- The glowing point at the top of the lighthouse should be white (#FFFFFF) or very pale teal
- Transparent background, exported as PNG
- Square canvas (1:1 aspect ratio), must remain legible and recognizable at small sizes (as small as 32x32px)
- Icon only — no text, no wordmark, no tagline
- Modern SaaS aesthetic — reference the icon style of Linear, Vercel, Supabase, or Raycast

Output: A single high-resolution icon (at least 512x512px) on a transparent background.
```
